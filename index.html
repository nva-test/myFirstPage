My Favorite things to do in Cape Town

There are a lot of fun things to do in and around Cape Town. Here are just some examples:

  • Hike Table Mountain
  • Go winetasting in Stellenbosch
  • Take the Red Bus

Hike Table Mountain

This picture is actually taken on top of Lion's Head, looking out on Table Mountain. Lion's Head is part of the Table Mountain National Park.

Go winetasting in Stellenbosch

This is my favorite wine farm in Stellenbosch. It has great wines, breathtaking views and good food.

Take the Red Bus

Although it might seem as a tourist trap, this Red Bus is actually a great way to see this amazing city. Even the locals love it!
